About EduVector

EduVector is an innovative educational platform designed to increase motivation and interest in learning. The platform solves engagement problems through gamified courses, interactive simulations, and AI assistants that make learning both engaging and personalized.

Built on Unity engine, EduVector is cross-platform and universal. It develops independence, critical thinking, and digital skills, helping students build their future through knowledge engagement.

Core Objectives

  • Develop critical thinking through practical simulations
  • Increase motivation through game mechanics
  • Provide adaptive learning for individual student needs
  • Integrate AI assistants for personalized education
